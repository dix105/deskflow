# DESIGN.md

## 1. Foundation
- Surface: warm paper `#f3efe4`
- Ink: near-black `#10100e`
- Muted text: `#5d594f`
- Accent blue: `#2458ff`
- Signal red: `#e23b2f`
- Success green: `#157c4f`

## 2. Typography
- Primary UI: IBM Plex Sans
- Technical labels: IBM Plex Mono
- Headlines use compressed tracking and strong weight, not decorative serif styling.

## 3. Layout
- Sidebar behaves like a dark instrument rail.
- Main content uses a paper workspace with large type and clear recording hierarchy.
- Cards should feel like labeled hardware panels, not generic floating SaaS cards.

## 4. Components
- Primary actions: ink background, white text, sharp 12px radius.
- Secondary actions: paper background, ink border.
- Inputs: white paper wells with firm dark focus rings.
- Pills/badges: mono labels, uppercase, compact.

## 5. Motion
- Motion is functional: recording pulse, hover lift, fast focus feedback.
- Avoid ornamental bouncing or mascot-like animation.

## 6. Do / Don't
- Do prioritize recording, shortcuts, provider state, glossary, and history.
- Do use high contrast and compact operator controls.
- Don't use vague gradients, generic pastel AI colors, or purely decorative cards.
