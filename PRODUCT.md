# PRODUCT.md

## Product
FlowDesk is a desktop dictation app for people who want speech to become usable text instantly across any app.

## Audience
Operators, founders, builders, and fast typists who dictate notes, replies, instructions, and product ideas while moving between tools.

## Promise
Speak once. FlowDesk captures, cleans, remembers, and pastes the right text back where work is happening.

## Brand voice
Calm, precise, tactile, fast. It should feel like a serious desktop instrument, not a playful chatbot toy.

## Anti-references
- Generic rounded SaaS dashboard
- Soft purple AI assistant clichés
- Overused 2025 AI landing page typography
- Decorative UI that slows down the core recording flow

## Design direction for Impeccable branch
A black-ink field recorder on warm paper: dense controls, editorial hierarchy, sharp panels, visible audio craft, and strong contrast.
